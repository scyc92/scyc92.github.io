Geolocation Proximity

  • Tech Stack: Python, Pandas, Geopy, Excel

Geolocation Proximity is designed to identify and rank individuals based on their proximity to specific store locations using geodesic distance calculations. By leveraging latitude and longitude coordinates, the project calculates the distance between each store and individual, then organizes the results to either display the closest person or provide a ranked list of all individuals by distance. The results are automatically exported to an Excel file for further use and analysis.
